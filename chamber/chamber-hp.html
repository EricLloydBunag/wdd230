About Us

Welcome to the Davao del Norte Chamber of Commerce and Industry, a vibrant community of entrepreneurs, innovators, and industry leaders committed to driving economic growth and prosperity in our province. With a focus on fostering strategic partnerships and proactive advocacy, we empower local businesses to thrive in a dynamic ecosystem of innovation and opportunity. Through a wide range of resources, networks, and support systems, including educational programs and networking events, we aim to fuel growth, enhance competitiveness, and stimulate economic progress. Join us on this journey of empowerment and transformation as we work together to build a brighter future for our businesses, our communities, and our nation.
