Discover the Province of Davao Del Norte

Welcome to the vibrant province of Davao Del Norte! Nestled in the heart of the Philippines, this enchanting region offers a rich tapestry of natural wonders, cultural heritage, and economic opportunities. With its stunning landscapes, diverse communities, and thriving industries, Davao Del Norte beckons visitors and investors alike to explore its boundless potential. Come and experience the warmth and hospitality of Davao Del Norte, where every visit is a journey to remember.

Events

  • Kadagayaan Festival: Davao de Norte's founding anniversary celebration on July 1st.
  • Durian Festival: A month long showcase of the durian planted bt the Plant Now Pay Later beneficiaries andother durian growers from August to September in Tagum City
  • Sinawug Festival: An annual thanksgiving of the lumads(natives) done in the river of Asuncion on September 19th.
